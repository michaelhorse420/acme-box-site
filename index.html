FAQ

What does Acme Box do?

Acme Box helps businesses save time and money by automating repetitive tasks with AI tools. We design, build, and maintain custom automated workflows for customer support, reporting, document processing, marketing content creation, and more.

How does AI automation work?

We use platforms like Make.com, Zapier, and custom AI models to connect your existing software and automate manual processes. This allows your business to run faster, with fewer errors, and without increasing staff costs.

Which industries do you work with?

We work with e‑commerce, logistics, marketing agencies, accounting firms, tourism, and professional services. If your business relies on repetitive digital tasks, we can likely automate them.

Can AI automation replace my employees?

Our goal isn’t to replace people, but to free them from repetitive, low‑value tasks so they can focus on high‑impact work. Automation supports your team — it doesn’t replace it.

How long does it take to set up an automation?

Simple automations can be set up within 1–3 days. More complex systems (e.g., full customer service automation or multi‑step reporting) typically take 1–3 weeks.

How much does it cost?

Setup starts from €500 for simple automations; complex solutions range from €2,000–€5,000. Monthly maintenance plans start at €100.

Do I need technical skills to use your systems?

No. Our solutions run in the background. Once set up, you just enjoy the results — no coding required.

Can you work with my existing software?

Yes. We integrate with Gmail, Google Sheets, Slack, HubSpot, Shopify, WordPress, and many more.

What’s the process to get started?

Step 1 — Book a free consultation. Step 2 — We analyse your processes and identify automation opportunities. Step 3 — We build and implement a custom AI automation plan. Step 4 — We monitor and maintain the system to keep it running smoothly.

Do you offer a free trial or demo?

Yes. We can build a mini‑demo using your data to show exactly how automation will work before you commit.
